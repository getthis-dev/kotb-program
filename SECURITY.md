# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in KOTB, please report it responsibly.

**Email:** getthis.dv@gmail.com

### What to include

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Any suggested fixes (optional)

### Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgment:** Within 48 hours
- **Initial Assessment:** Within 7 days
- **Resolution:** Depends on severity

### Scope

This policy applies to:

- The KOTB smart contract (Solana program)
- Related infrastructure

### Out of Scope

- Frontend UI bugs (unless they lead to fund loss)
- Social engineering attacks
- Denial of service attacks

## Rewards

We may offer bounties for critical vulnerabilities at our discretion. Please contact us for details.

## Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

- Act in good faith
- Avoid privacy violations
- Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond proof-of-concept
- Report the issue promptly
